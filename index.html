Portfolio

Examples of our work, turning structural problems into buildable realities.

  • Heritage Case

    Compromised Historic Timber Stabilization

    The Challenge: Shifting foundation geometry on a 17th-century framework.

    The Solution: Hidden custom reinforcement brackets engineered to take the dead load without ruining visual continuity.

  • Industrial

    Industrial support works

    The Challenge: New liquid nitrogen store at the University of Manchester.

    The Solution: Steel columns supporting primary longitudinal beams, with secondary transverse joists/spanning between the primary beams to support the mass of new nitrogen tanks and liquid nitrogen at 808.5 kg/m³.

  • Commercial

    Structural alteration for a new Footlocker store

    The Challenge: Opening the second floor and assocated structural works out a new Footlocker store in the Baltic Triangle in Liverpool.

    The Solution: A section of the existing concrete floor was removed to create the new opening, with temporary works supporting the structure during removal. New structural steel beams and columns were then installed to transfer the retained floor and wall loads safely around the enlarged opening.

The UK Authority on Plywood Box Beam Construction

Plywood box beams offer incredible strength-to-weight ratios, sustainable profiles, and spectacular architectural aesthetics. As the nation's sole dedicated specialist, we bridge rigorous academic research with hands-on site deployment.

"Our team houses proprietary research and custom testing data that allows us to clear plywood installations where others say it's structurally impossible."

Why Plywood Beams?

  • Unmatched Lightweight Strength: Ideal for complex modifications.
  • Low Embodied Carbon: Truly sustainable technical design.
  • Creative Freedom: Clean spans without massive steel footprints.
