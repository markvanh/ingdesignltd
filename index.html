Portfolio

Examples of our work, turning structural problems into buildable realities.

  • Public realm

    Beachfront huts

    The Challenge: As with all the artistic projects we support, we sympathetically designed the structure that supports these large scale wooden spheres without compromising their artistic integrity.

    The Solution: Engineered a low-profile internal steel support framing assembly to handle cthe enter-of-mass safely while preserving clean, uncompromised exterior artistic lines.

  • Industrial

    Industrial framing

    The Challenge: New liquid nitrogen store at the University of Manchester.

    The Solution: Steel columns supporting primary longitudinal beams, with secondary transverse joists/spanning between the primary beams to support the mass of new nitrogen tanks and liquid nitrogen at 808.5 kg/m³.

  • Commercial

    Structural alterations for Footlocker

    The Challenge: Creating a new double height space with associated structural works for a new Footlocker store in the Baltic Triangle, Liverpool.

    The Solution: Specification of temporay works prior to removal of a section of existing concrete floor to create the new double height space. New structural steel beams and columns were then installed to transfer the retained floor and wall loads around the enlarged opening.

  • Commercial

    warehouse extension

    The Challenge: Opening the second floor and associated structural works for a new Footlocker store in the Baltic Triangle in Liverpool.

    The Solution: A section of the existing concrete floor was removed to create the new opening, with temporary works supporting the structure during removal. New structural steel beams and columns were then installed to transfer the retained floor and wall loads safely around the enlarged opening.

  • Domestic

    Large house extension

    The Challenge: The client wanted to extend their house by adding a large groundfloor extension that replaced an existing groundfloor structure.

    The Solution: Part of the existing structure was removed safely and a steel-franed extension built to ING Design specifications.

  • Heritage

    Structural refurbishment

    The Challenge: A repeat client wanted to refurbish a structurally compromised set of three Victorian stables.

    The Solution: We performed comprehensive structural survey, identifed problem areas, and specified remedial works. As per the client's request, wherever possible, we focused on structural remediation approaches that he could implement himself. We continue our involvement, providing ongoing support and advice as the building is refurbished.

The UK Authority on Plywood Box Beam Construction

Plywood box beams offer incredible strength-to-weight ratios, sustainable profiles, and spectacular architectural aesthetics. As the nation's sole dedicated specialist, we bridge rigorous academic research with hands-on site deployment.

"Our team houses proprietary research and custom testing data that allows us to clear plywood installations where others say it's structurally impossible."

Why Plywood Beams?

  • Unmatched Lightweight Strength: Ideal for complex modifications.
  • Low Embodied Carbon: Truly sustainable technical design.
  • Creative Freedom: Clean spans without massive steel footprints.
